Our Donate Plan

If we want to make this world a better place than donating to the nonprofit organizations and charities is the best solution. When people will get employed they will not become criminals and so our world will be filled with positivity.

Child focused and community based

We tackle poverty at its roots. Our integrated approach includes water, healthcare, education, child protection, and income generation, so every child can grow into who God created them to be.

Education

Education transforms lives and breaks the cycle of poverty that traps so many children. Education for girls is essential — an educated mother will make sure her children go to and stay in school.

Food For A Family

Tackling hunger on the frontline of emergencies is critical, but we also need long-lasting solutions. That's why we work with countries around the world to help vulnerable people everywhere get the food they need to survive and thrive.
